Insights to Automotive Company on Electric Vehicles launch in India

This data-driven project focuses on understanding the electric vehicle (EV) market landscape in India. Analyzed EV and hybrid sales trends, penetration rates, and market potential across states, vehicle categories, and manufacturers. Leveraging tools like Power BI, Snowflake, and Python, our insights to expand in the Indian market, where their EV market share currently stands at under 2%. This analysis supports informed decision-making and highlights growth opportunities.

Tools used: PostgreSQL, Power Query and Power BI
